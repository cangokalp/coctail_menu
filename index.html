Cocktails

French 75

This cocktail is very easy to drink, about as hard to dislike as Christmas. This very simple and elegant cocktail includes gin, a bit of lemon juice and simple syrup accompanied with champagne/dry sparkling wine. Served in a highball over ice.

New York Sour

The origin of this classic cocktail is misty, some say it's possibly invented in Chicago around 19th Century, and then later renamed by a New Yorker. The red wine float on top is the main differentiator of this clssic from a well known Whiskey Sour. As the new year approaches, keep an open mind and try this cocktail that has a rather contraversial combination of red wine and whiskey at first glance. The wine creates a juicy and to a slight extent tannic bite, plus the two-tone color scheme is mesmerizing!
