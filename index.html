About Me

Hi! My name is Can Gokalp, and I am a Ph.D. student studying Operations Research at the University of Texas at Austin. I am aiming to graduate by May 2021. My research mainly involves Network Optimization - the kind of problems I worked on are listed under the experience section below.

Aside from research, I'm fascinated by CNN, NLP and RL applications. In 2017-2018, I worked as a Data Science Intern at SparkCognition. That was the time I got very interested in Deep Learning. My work has been mostly focused on research/implementation of Reinforcement Learning algorithms and also Evolutionary Algorithms to showcase the strength of a SparkCognition product that is similar in function to AutoML. For fun I often take on a fun project and build AI agents to entertain myself. I'm currently working on training a Char-RNN model to generate lyrics similar to Lonely Island songs

Outside of career pursuits, I enjoy balancing time on the computer with sports. I played college soccer in Turkey for 4 years, and I now play in the Austin Co-ed Soccer League and the intramural tournaments at UT Austin. I'm a big fan of Galatasaray (a Turkish soccer team), when they have a game on the weekends that becomes my first priority :).
